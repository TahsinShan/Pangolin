How to Reduce the Employee Turnover Rate at Your Company

Pangolin – Detailed Solutions

1

On-Time Wage Access

What it is: A payroll system that ensures salaries are disbursed on time, every time, without deductions or manipulation. Salaries flow digitally from the company account to the worker’s Pangolin wallet, reducing reliance on cash-handling middlemen.

Why it matters for workers: Delayed or cut wages cause stress, debt cycles, and force workers to borrow at predatory rates. With Pangolin, workers can plan their family expenses, pay bills, and avoid loan sharks.

Why it matters for companies: On-time wages build trust, reduce disputes, and improve retention and productivity. Factories also gain regulatory compliance and digital payroll records for audits and reporting.

2

Emergency Microloans

What it is: Instant, small, low-interest loans available directly through the Pangolin app when salaries are delayed or emergencies strike (medical, school fees, family needs).

Why it matters for workers: Workers usually borrow from local moneylenders at 120–200% interest annually. Pangolin loans are ethical, transparent, and repayable directly from upcoming wages. This reduces exploitation and protects dignity.

Why it matters for companies: Financially stable workers are less absent, more focused, and loyal. This feature prevents strikes and walkouts when salaries are delayed, giving employers operational continuity.

3

Digital Leave Requests

What it is: A mobile feature where workers can apply for leave using ready-made templates (sick leave, family emergency, maternity, etc.) or directly call their supervisor from within the app.

Why it matters for workers: Many workers fear asking for leave verbally due to power dynamics with supervisors. Digital requests create a paper trail, ensuring fairness and reducing favoritism.

Why it matters for companies: HR gains structured leave records, reducing confusion and last-minute absenteeism. Transparent leave policies improve morale and reduce disputes.

4

Attendance & Productivity Tracking

What it is: Workers clock in/out via QR code scans or biometric devices. Break times and overtime are logged automatically, feeding into payroll and performance dashboards.

Why it matters for workers: Workers can see their actual working hours, overtime, and breaks in real time — ensuring they are paid correctly. No more manipulation or undercounting.

Why it matters for companies: Managers gain accurate attendance and productivity data. This improves workforce planning, reduces fraud (ghost workers), and ensures fair overtime payouts.

5

Cash-Out via Local Vendors

What it is: A network of trusted community shops and vendors that act as withdrawal points. Workers can cash out wages without ATMs, bank accounts, or high mobile wallet fees.

Why it matters for workers: Banking exclusion is common in rural Bangladesh. With Pangolin, workers can access their full salary easily, avoiding the 8–10% cash-out fees from mobile money operators.

Why it matters for companies: Factories avoid bottlenecks of cash distribution days and reduce risks of carrying large sums of cash. It also boosts goodwill as workers see real savings in their take-home pay.

6

Job Matching Tool

What it is: A platform feature connecting workers to job opportunities in partner factories/companies, based on skill, location, and experience.

Why it matters for workers: Many workers find jobs through informal, exploitative middlemen. Pangolin provides a direct, transparent channel, empowering workers to negotiate better opportunities.

Why it matters for companies: Employers gain access to a reliable talent pool with verified work history, reducing recruitment costs and turnover.

🎯 Strategic Initiatives

  • Ensure Fair Wages: Create a transparent wage ecosystem that prevents theft, cuts, or manipulation. Builds worker dignity and international compliance reputation for factories.
  • Reduce Turnover & Improve Productivity: Timely wages + microloans = less stress, less absenteeism, more output. Helps companies save money on rehiring and training.
  • Build Trust Between Owners & Workers: Both sides see the same data (wages, hours, leave), reducing disputes. Increases workplace harmony and reduces conflict-driven slowdowns.
  • Empower Worker Dignity: Workers gain financial independence and a voice in workplace decisions. Strengthens social reputation of companies (good for global buyers & NGOs).
  • Strengthen HR for Companies: One integrated system for payroll, attendance, leave, compliance, and loans. Saves HR departments time and money, while improving regulatory compliance.

⚡️ Together, these solutions make Pangolin more than just a payments tool — it’s a worker dignity + productivity platform designed for Bangladesh and scalable across Asia.
